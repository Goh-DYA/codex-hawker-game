## Evidence boundary

- Current expanded source defines 12 stall identities and 46 dish presentations with unique primary visual references, alongside the existing 80 distinct placeable contracts and 12 customer archetypes. Automated contracts do not establish realism, cultural accuracy, or human approval.
- The stall upgrade targets layered code-native roof, fascia, sign, wall, service-window, counter, equipment, and trim treatments; food and drink props must come from each placed stall's active menu rather than a generic cuisine-colour substitute.
- Every open stall is intended to contain a code-native vendor whose deterministic idle and cuisine-appropriate preparation/serving actions remain behind the counter. Closing the centre must replace the service scene with an opaque shutter, and reduced motion must freeze the vendor in a readable static work pose without changing service timing.
- Fresh automated coverage verifies all 12 unique vendor identities, tools, emblems, deterministic idle/order/preparation poses, reduced-motion invariants, and active-menu prop selection. A 1280 × 720 development-browser pass inspected Sunrise Roost, Coconut & Lime, compact Kopi Canopy, and 5 × 3 Harbour Ember together, including a one-to-three dish live menu change, open and opaque-closed states, standard/lower-end quality, normal/high contrast, and full/reduced motion. No game console warning or error was observed.
- On bundled Node.js `v24.14.0`, the expanded source passes typecheck, lint, 24 Vitest files / 131 tests, the five-environment Sites/Vinext build, 2/2 rendered-output/offline-shell tests, and the native Vercel build.
- Current local-browser passes inspected the expanded persona roster, seven live mixed visitors, the Settings customer-status guide, and the preferred-route editor with no client-console errors. At 1024 × 640, the route editor exposed its legend and controls, paused and resumed correctly, accepted keyboard painting, cleared and undid the guide, and restored it after reload. Eight retained screenshots from the earlier development build sample queue and expansion behavior but do not exercise the four new stalls or cover all 46 dishes.
- The screenshots also predate the final audit fixes for globally unique queue anchors, live-order-preserving reroutes, utility-adjusted meal depletion, and the restored route overlay. They are targeted evidence, not performance, accessibility, cultural, or exhaustive graphics approval.
- The expanded build generated service-worker build ID `c5ed3679ba56`. Current payload totals and deterministic manifest hash were not retained in this validation update; older artifact measurements remain explicitly historical in `PERFORMANCE_REPORT.md`.
- The renderer still performs a full-frame redraw and recreates text during frame rendering. Automated recipe tests and the Node simulation benchmark do not measure that browser CPU/allocation path.
